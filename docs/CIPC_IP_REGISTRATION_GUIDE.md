# CIPC Intellectual Property & Registration Roadmap
**Project:** The Exchange | Premium Verified Marketplace
**Jurisdiction:** South Africa

This document outlines the necessary steps and technical details required for protecting "The Exchange" against competitors and unauthorized replication under South African law (Copyright Act, Trademarks Act, and ECTA).

---

## 1. Company Registration (CIPC)
To legally own the IP, "The Exchange" should be registered as a private company (Pty) Ltd.
- **Form:** COR 14.1 (Notice of Incorporation)
- **Proposed Name:** The Exchange Marketplace (Pty) Ltd
- **Business Category:** Information Technology / E-commerce Platform

## 2. Trademark Registration (TM1)
To prevent competitors from using your name, colors, or "style," you must register a Trademark.
- **Class 35:** Advertising; business management; organization of a marketplace for buyers and sellers of goods and services.
- **Class 42:** Software as a Service (SaaS); platform for identity verification; AI-powered market analytics.
- **Assets to Protect:**
    - The name: "THE EXCHANGE"
    - The tagline: "The smartest way to trade locally."
    - The visual identity: The specific combination of #225BC3 (Dependable Blue) and #34CBED (Aqua).

## 3. Copyright Protection (Automatic but Documented)
In South Africa, software code is protected as a "Literary Work" under the Copyright Act. To prove authorship in a dispute:
- **Proof of Authorship:** This repository and its commit history serve as the primary evidence.
- **Technical Assets:**
    - **Original Codebase:** Next.js 15 App Router architecture.
    - **Proprietary AI Logic:** Custom Genkit flows for Biometric KYC and Scam Detection.
    - **Database Architecture:** The specific Firestore schema defined in `backend.json`.
    - **UI/UX Design:** The unique "Card-based" premium marketplace layout.

## 4. Anti-Competition Strategy
To prevent "Look-alike" apps:
- **Trade Secrets:** The specific weights and logic used in the "Reliability Score" algorithm.
- **User Database:** Protected under POPIA as a proprietary asset.
- **Unique Process:** The "Safe-Zone Meetup + Arrival Tracker" workflow is a unique business process that should be documented as a proprietary method.
